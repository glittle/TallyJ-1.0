Change History

TallyJ was first made available in 2002. Since then a number of corrections and enhancements have been made. TallyJ will continue to evolve as new features are added and shortcomings overcome. If you have ideas or suggestions about how to improve TallyJ, please go to the TallyJ Page on Facebook and record your ideas in the discussion board.
